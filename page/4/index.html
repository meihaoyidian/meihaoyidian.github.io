Activity B

Assess the Response of Different Soil P Pools and Crop Yield to Long-term P fertilization

1.The relative increase in soil P size from total P< oxalate P< Olsen P < CaCl2 P (increase in more available forms)
2.Olsen P and Oxalate P come at saturation at a P surplus near 3400 kg P/ha
3.When P surplus was nearly 2000 kg P/ha, added P did not fully accumulate and was lost by leaching
Implications for P management: A small step contributes to what is the real soil P dynamic under long term P fertilization at field conditions. Thus, for Qiyang site when the accumulated P was more than roughly 3400 kg P/ha, further P application wont contribute more available P, instead, high environmental risk came out. Besides, the classification help farmers know how much fertilizer they need further apply to reach their target levels for achieving optimal yields.
